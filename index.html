A social app to do social good


Givety brings friends together to make a difference. We’ve made it easy, fun and convenient for teens to volunteer and make an impact on their communities.


Just download the app, sign up for volunteer opportunities, invite others and start doing good with friends. Givety even tracks volunteer hours and notifies their school.


PARENTS


Givety’s centralized listing of non-profit organizations and volunteer opportunities makes it easy for teens to make a difference. And getting credit for their volunteering is easy – they can self-check-in electronically at the volunteer location when they arrive and check out when they’re done. No more paper forms requiring signatures.


By emphasizing the social of volunteering and providing varied and diverse volunteer opportunities — including last minute sign-ups —Givety encourages teens to get involved in their communities.

NON PROFITS


We’ve turned a teen’s favorite device into a tool to do social good — a tool to help non-profit organizations focus on their mission instead of recruiting volunteers.


Givety encourages teens to do community service with friends by making it a social activity. It supports the work of non-profits by bringing a new generation of volunteers to their door.

SCHOOLS


Givety encourages the digital generation to complete community service by making it fun and social and by providing volunteer options at their fingertips.


It makes the job of administrators easier by tracking volunteer hours with GPS and emailing them logs. Leveraging mobile technology, Givety provides the school with an electronic database of students’ participation, reducing workload and providing accurate data for college applications.

ABOUT


Tracey Schuster is the mom of a teen-aged girl and the founder of Givety.


Her goal was to foster the volunteering spirit of today’s busy, distracted teens, providing them with a tool to become responsible citizens.


She believes that by leveraging mobile technology and teens’ inherent desire to be social, Givety can help her daughter, and all teens, change the face of community service.


She looks forward to the day that volunteering is another fun, social activity instead of just a school requirement.
