Making a difference one friend at a time


Help your friends get involved, ask friends to support your efforts, work together to make a difference, and be recognized for your good deeds - Givety is the place to do all of that!


PARENTS

Givety’s centralized listing of nonprofit organizations and volunteer opportunities will help your child make a difference. And getting credit for their volunteer work is easy – they can self-check-in electronically at your volunteer location when they arrive and check out when they’re done. No more paper forms or getting signatures on time-sheets.


By emphasizing the social aspects of volunteering and bringing varied and diverse opportunities in which to do so, Givety will motivate students to get involved!

NON PROFITS


Givety’s community of next-generation givers helps you access a new pool of enthusiastic volunteers and increases your outreach.


We make volunteering and charitable activity more attractive for young people by making it social, more visible and easier to take part in.


By encouraging more people to take action while reducing the burden on your organization, you will be better able to pursue your mission and assist your clients.

SCHOOLS

Our tools streamline the process of tracking student volunteering by eliminating printed forms. Leveraging mobile technology, your institution will be able to build an accurate electronic database of time spent by students at nonprofits, reducing your workload and providing better data for college and other applications.


By emphasizing the social aspects of volunteering and bringing varied and diverse opportunities in which to do so, Givety will motivate students to get involved.

ABOUT


I’m Tracey Schuster and I’m the founder of Givety.


My goal for givety is to leverage mobile technologies and the internet to foster the volunteering spirit in students. I want to help kids become responsible citizens and change the face of community service to meet the needs of teen today.


If we engage kids at a younger age with a mobile social platform, volunteering can become another social activity instead of a chore.

Help us make Givety the go-to source for student volunteerism


You and your friends can see the places you’re helping out, find other interesting and exciting volunteering opportunities, and take part in it together.
